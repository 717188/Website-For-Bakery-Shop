🧁 Project Overview

Bun & Been Bakery Website is a modern and user-friendly online bakery platform.
It is designed to help customers easily explore and order bakery products such as cakes, pastries, and bread items.

The website also includes information about the bakery, contact options, and a smooth ordering experience.

✨ Features

🍰 Product Catalog

Displays a variety of bakery items (cakes, pastries, bread)
Each product includes image, description, and price
Easy and clean browsing experience

🛒 Online Ordering System

Add products to cart
View order summary
Place orders easily through the website

📱 Responsive Design

Works smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices
Clean and user-friendly interface

🥯 About & Contact Section

Shows bakery story and background
Contact form for inquiries and custom cake orders

🎨 Attractive UI

Warm bakery-themed design
High-quality product images
Simple and elegant layout
